About Super Poo

Dungdale's #1 Defender

Super Poo is a flying, swirl-shaped poop with a heart of gold and a cape that never quits! He swoops through the skies of Dungdale keeping everyone safe from villains and mischief.

Sporting his signature red boots and golden belt, Super Poo is always ready for action. Whether he's battling Super Spider or helping a friend in need, there's no challenge too big (or too stinky) for Dungdale's bravest hero.
